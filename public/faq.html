Frequently Asked Questions

❓ How do I list a property?

Log in to your landlord account, go to the dashboard, fill in the property details (name, location, price, photos, etc.), and click "Upload House". Your listing will appear on the public page after admin approval (or immediately if auto‑approved).

❓ Is there a fee to list?

Basic listing is free. You can pay to become a verified landlord (official or premium) or to feature a house for better visibility.

❓ How do tenants contact me?

Each listing displays your WhatsApp number. Tenants can click the link to chat directly. We also have an in‑app messaging system (coming soon).

❓ How do I reset my password?

On the login page, click "Forgot password?" and follow the instructions. A reset link will be sent to your email.

❓ How do I report a fake listing?

If you are logged in, click the "Report" button on the property card, provide a reason, and the admin will review it.

❓ I'm an admin – how do I manage the site?

Use the admin login page with your credentials. You'll see a dashboard with stats, and you can verify landlords, delete houses, and resolve reports.
