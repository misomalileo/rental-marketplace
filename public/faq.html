Frequently Asked Questions

How do I list a property?
Log in to your landlord account, go to the dashboard, fill in the property details (name, location, price, photos, etc.), and click "Upload House". Your listing will appear on the public page after approval (or instantly if auto‑approved).
Is there a fee to list?
Basic listing is free. You can pay to become a verified landlord (official or premium) for added trust badges. You may also pay to feature a house for better visibility.
How do tenants contact me?
Each listing displays your WhatsApp number – tenants can click the link to chat directly. We also have an in‑app messaging system (accessible from the chat page) for logged‑in users.
How do I reset my password?
On the login page, click "Forgot password?" and follow the instructions. A reset link will be sent to your registered email.
How do I report a fake listing?
If you are logged in, click the "Report" button on the property card, provide a reason, and the admin will review it promptly.
I'm an admin – how do I manage the site?
Use the admin login page with your credentials. You'll see a dashboard with stats, and you can verify landlords, delete houses, and resolve reports.
Can I pay with Airtel Money or TNM Mpamba?
Yes! We support Airtel Money, TNM Mpamba, and Standard Bank. Payments are processed securely through PayChangu.
How do I generate a digital lease agreement?
After a booking is confirmed, both landlord and tenant can generate a lease agreement from the booking details. You can sign it digitally using the signature pad.
What is a virtual tour and how do I add one?
Virtual tours let tenants explore your property in 360°. You can upload a 360° image or a YouTube/Vimeo walkthrough video when creating or editing a listing. Tenants will see a "Virtual Tour" tab on the property modal.
